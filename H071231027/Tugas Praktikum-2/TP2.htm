About

I am Kevin Ardhana, an undergraduate student in the Information Systems program at Universitas Hasanuddin, Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences. With a passion for IT and entrepreneurship, I have honed my skills through various leadership and project experiences. I led the KPMP MIPA science community and headed the Skills Division at Pandu Digital. Additionally, I achieved 2nd place in the Young Entrepreneurship competition by Ruangguru. My portfolio includes designing the AgroVerify app in a UI/UX competition and contributing to various tech projects such as web and app development, data analysis, and podcast operations. I am actively involved in the startup ecosystem and aspire to further grow as a youthpreneur. Currently, I also work part-time as a blogger, sharing insights on tech and innovation.
